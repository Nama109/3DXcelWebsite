Get Involved

Are you a student or an individual interested in exploring the world of 3D printing and CAD?
Here are some ways you can get involved with 3D Print for All

Apply Here

Attend our free classes: Join our introductory courses to learn the basics of 3D printing and CAD software. Whether you're a complete beginner or have some experience, our classes are designed to cater to different skill levels.

Volunteer your expertise: If you have experience in 3D printing, CAD design, or teaching, consider volunteering your time and knowledge to help others. By becoming a volunteer instructor or mentor, you can make a significant impact on someone's journey.

Donate or sponsor: Help us continue providing free education and access to 3D printing technology by making a donation or sponsoring our programs. Your support enables us to expand our reach and empower more individuals in their creative pursuits.
